Image Optimization

Logo Image

For the logo, I first converted the image to greyscale. I then went went through GIF, JPEG, and PNG in order to see which one gave me the smallest file size. I ended up going with PNG, and had a final size of 3.70 KB.

Bridge Image

This image was a little bit more involved. I knew that I couldn't turn the image greyscale, so I decided to just convert it to JPEG, lower the quality, and increase the blur to make the artifacting less apparent. The final size was 29.6 KB.
