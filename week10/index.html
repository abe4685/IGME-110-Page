Magazine Cover

I wasn't really sure what topic to choose, so I just decided to do something based on the game I'm currently playing, Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice. I knew I wanted the image to have a border, as I wanted to play around with some kind of pseudo-3d effect, but after I did that I realized that it looked more like a poster than a magazine. I also realized that I needed more images than just the one, so I added another and made them blend together using photo masks. I also knew I wanted the stylized japanese title somewhere in the image, but I could only find bad quality versions of it. To work around this, I traced it by hand and remade it from scratch. Lastly, I wanted to make it look at least a little like a magazine, so I added the PC Gamer logo. I kinda regret not adding more text to the image, but it is what it is.
